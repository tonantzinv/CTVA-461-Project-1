Guerrero

You have to try this one first!

Guerrero is located in the southern part of Mexico. Some of its famous cities include Acapulco, Zihuatanejo, and Ixtapa. Many tourists still flock to this 1990s vacation destination to escape reality. Acapulco is surrounded by many other smaller cities and even smaller towns. There are beaches that are more private than others, there’s cliff diver’s that are famous for jumping off of high cliffs for tourist to watch, some even jump with fire into the rocky waters that lie below.

In Downtown Acapulco The water parks have so many rides to choose from. Restaurants having tables on the beach just like Sayulita, tourists can enjoy catching a bite to eat and play in the warm water while you’re at it.
